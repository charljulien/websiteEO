The Team

The Bitch

Computing science genius constantly exploring new numerical arts boundaries, the Bitch, alias Roman, is from Paris. I know, nobody's perfect. He brings the best of his talent to bitchslap us and accompany Electric Offense to new heights

De echte Brusseleir

His dudeness, Antoun Di Tounzi, plays as a resident DJ with Roman as UltraSex. He's an engineer in electric components and is currently composing his first tracks. He's the idealisation of the Retro 80's cyberpunk living among the tunes of synthwave, neons and laser raptors
